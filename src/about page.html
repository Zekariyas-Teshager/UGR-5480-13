About Us

Don Bosco Catholic High School is a school created by a person named Don Bosco, who has been through many things in his life. He created this school to provide education for children, who can't get this education because of many reasons like economical problem, family problem and others.

Our vision

Our vision is to be the best school in our subcity, and in addition to that, we want to give students a quality education with state-of-the-art educational materials. Not only that, but we want to provide our students with many activities to participate in.

Our motto

"Quality education for quality students" signifies our dream as well as our purpose. It is the slogan that inspires students to participate in the great adventure of education.

Our staff

We have highly trained staffs, whether in class or out of class, dedicated to answering any questions that students or parents alike may have. They are delicate in their teaching, graceful in their explanations, and motivating in their work. Students will have an easy time interacting with our staff.
